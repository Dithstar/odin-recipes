PEANUT BUTTER BLOSSOMS

Home Page

What is this cookie?

A Peanut Butter Blossom is a peanut butter sugar cookie base with a dark chocolate Hershey's Kiss nestled on top.

Tools Needed

  • Large bowl
  • Medium bowl
  • Whisk
  • Spatula
  • Baking sheet
  • Cooling rack

Ingredients

  • Unbleached all-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Granulated sugar
  • Light brown sugar
  • Room temperature salted butter
  • Creamy peanut butter
  • Eggs
  • Pure vanilla extract
  • Milk
  • Dark chocolate Hershey's Kiss

STEPS

  1. Whisk together the dry ingredients in the medium bowl and set aside.
  2. Cream together the butter, peanut butter and sugar.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla.
  4. Slowly add in the dry ingredients, making sure not to over mix.
  5. Add the milk and fold a couple more times.
  6. Make dough balls using 1-2 tbsp of dough.
  7. Set the oven to 350F and then put in the dough when preheated. Baked for 7-8 minutes.
  8. Dough should be starting to brown when you take them out. Quickly add a Hershey's kiss to each center and then transfer to a baking rack to cool.
